About OpenAi

OpenAI is an artificial intelligence (AI) research company, that is advertised as non-profit, and that aims to promote and develop friendly artificial intelligence in such a way that, they say, benefits humanity as a whole.
Contrary,its development is not based on any open community nor does it provide any free software.
The organization aims to "collaborate freely" with other institutions and researchers when doing their patents and research open to the public.
